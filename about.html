About Orion Carbon

We are a black female-owned sole proprietorship focused on blockchain-based carbon credit issuance and trading.

Business Model Canvas Overview

1. Key Partners

Large pollution emitters (e.g., Sasol), environmental NGOs, blockchain developers

2. Key Activities

Issuing carbon credits, trading platform management, blockchain integration

3. Key Resources

Blockchain infrastructure, regulatory licenses, skilled personnel

4. Value Propositions

Transparent carbon credit tracking, efficient trading, sustainable impact

5. Customer Relationships

Dedicated account management, platform support, education on carbon credits

6. Channels

Website, blockchain platform, email communication, workshops

7. Customer Segments

Large industrial emitters, investors in carbon markets, government entities

8. Cost Structure

Platform development, blockchain fees, staff salaries, marketing

9. Revenue Streams

Trading fees, subscription fees, consulting services
