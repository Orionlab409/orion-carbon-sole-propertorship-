About Orion Carbon

Orion Carbon is a black female-owned sole proprietorship focused on blockchain-based carbon credit issuance and trading. We deliver transparency, efficiency, and measurable impact for large-scale carbon markets.

Key Partners

Large industrial emitters (Sasol), environmental NGOs, blockchain developers

Key Activities

Issuing carbon credits, managing trading platform, integrating blockchain

Key Resources

Blockchain infrastructure, regulatory licenses, skilled personnel

Value Propositions

Transparent carbon credit tracking, efficient trading, sustainable impact

Customer Relationships

Dedicated account management, platform support, education on carbon credits

Channels

Website, blockchain platform, email communication, workshops

Customer Segments

Large industrial emitters, investors in carbon markets, government entities

Cost Structure

Platform development, blockchain fees, staff salaries, marketing

Revenue Streams

Trading fees, subscription fees, consulting services
