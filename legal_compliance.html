Demonstrating **Transparency** and adherence to **Excellence** through comprehensive documentation. Last Updated: November 2025.

1. Privacy Policy

Orion Carbon ("We," "Us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use our services, website, or blockchain platform.

Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as name, email, and contact number (e.g., during inquiry submissions or MRV Academy enrollment).

Blockchain Data

Please note that data related to carbon credit transactions (e.g., wallet addresses, transaction hashes, token balances) recorded on the public ledger are inherently transparent and immutable. We only link this public data to private user identity as required for regulatory compliance.

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All team communications related to sensitive data adhere to strict, zero-trust protocols.
