Three Integrated Divisions

1. Carbon Credit Development

“We turn South African emission-reducing projects into verified carbon credits that meet national MRV and Carbon Budget standards.”

Customers: Large emitters, renewable developers, municipalities, NGOs
Revenue: R25k–R100k dev fee + R12k–R30k annual + 10–20% success fee
Year 1: 5 projects → ~R450k
Partners: SGS, BlueNorth, Municipalities, Green Asset Exchange

2. Tokenization & Marketplace

“We convert verified carbon credits into blockchain-backed digital assets that cannot be duplicated, lost, or double-counted.”

Customers: Emitters, ESG investors, project owners
Revenue: R5–R20 per credit + 0.5–1.5% trading fee
Year 1: 10,000 credits → ~R100k
Partners: Polygon/Celo, GAE, JSE, Xpansiv

3. Orion Academy

“We train South African youth to perform carbon monitoring and reporting, giving them immediate access to green jobs.”

Customers: Unemployed youth, corporates, municipalities
Revenue: Course fees + corporate training + grants
Year 1: 100 learners → ~R100k
Partners: SETA, universities, NGOs
